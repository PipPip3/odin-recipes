The Perfect Poached Egg

How to Perfectly Poach an Egg

Description:

The humble poached egg is the cornerstone of chef skill tests across the world. Use this recipe to help improve your future poached eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups of water in a pot
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of white vinegar
  • 1 slice of your favorite bread, toasted and buttered

Directions:

  1. Bring the water to a rolling boil
  2. Crack the egg into a cup
  3. Add the salt, pepper, and white vinegar to the boiling water
  4. Whisk the water for 30 seconds until a strong circular current of water has developed
  5. Drop the egg into the center of the boiling water
  6. Allow the egg to spin in the water for 90 to 120 seconds
  7. Gently emove the poached egg from the water and place on to paper towel
  8. Dab the egg with paper wowel to remove any excess water
  9. Place the egg on to the buttered toast
  10. Season with salt and pepper
