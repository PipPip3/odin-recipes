The Flawlessly Roasted Fish

How to Cook a Flawlessly Roasted Fish

Description:

Follow this simple roasted fish recipe to wow the judges of your next cooking test!

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole fish
  • Olive oil
  • Minced fresh garlic
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 lemon per fish
  • Fresh herbs, such as rosemary and thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Rinse and pat the fish dry
  2. Score the fish
  3. Brush the fish thoroughly with oil, inside and out
  4. Stuff the cavity with the herbs, lemon, and minced garlic
  5. Add salt and pepper to the inside of the fish
  6. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder to the outside of the fish
  7. Roast the fish for 18-20 minutes at 450Â°F or until it flakes easily with a fork
  8. Remove the fish from the oven
  9. Add lemon juice and serve immediately
