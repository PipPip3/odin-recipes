The Splendid Souffle

How to Cook a Splendid Souffle

Description:

One thing is guaranteed, as a savory or sweet dish, the soft and fluffly Splendid Souffle will put your cooking skills to the test.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Caster Sugar to line souffle dishes
  • 50g butter
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup caster sugar

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. Grease four ramekin dishes and evenly sprinkle the inside of the dish with caster sugar. Shake off any excess.
  3. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over a medium heat
  4. Add flour and stir to combine with butter until the mixture thickens
  5. Remove from heat, add chocolate, and egg yolks.
  6. Stir to fully combine the mixture and transfer to a large bowl
  7. Add the egg white to a separate bowl
  8. Beat the egg whites using an electric mixer until soft peaks form
  9. Slowly add caster sugar while continuing to beat the egg whites
  10. Beat the mixture until it is fully combined
  11. Add the egg ehite mixture to the chocolate mixture and fold through until combined
  12. Spoon the mixture into the ramekin bowls
  13. Cook in the oven for about 15 minutes or until the souffles are puffed up, cooked through, and brown on top
  14. Remove from the oven
  15. Sprinkle the tops with icing sugar and serve
